ABOUT US


REVAMP is a new-age educational platform established to revamp the mindset and financial IQ of common man by creating awareness about how to achieve financial freedom, correcting the cognitive blueprint and teaching how to leverage time, effort & money. Financial learning is a crucial aspect of education among diverse sector and we at Revamp have embarked upon this endeavor of paramount importance. A person can be highly educated, professionally successful but financially illiterate, alas! Revampers firmly believe “success is not about making money, it’s about making the man who will by default make money.”


Vision

"Our Vision is to revamp an individual to achieve financial freedom through heuristic learning & holistic development."


Mission

To create a global learning ecosystem that will provide exposure to individuals in various areas like Financial Education, Leadership, Entrepreneurship, People Management, Mindset, and many more that are a imperative to become financially free by working through partnerships with like-minded individuals, corporations & organizations.
